DISTRIBUTION OF GUN VIOLENCE IN UNITED STATES

About Me  Visualization  Credits  SVG CANVAS  BLACK HAT  WHITE HAT 

The map above shows the number of deaths due to guns in the violence.

The darker color indicates more death and the lighter colors indicate more death.
California has the most number of deaths followed by Texas. FLorida and Chicago come 3rd and 4th respectively.
